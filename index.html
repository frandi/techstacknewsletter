Go Wandering: Cost Saving With Redis Cache

Redis is known as a distributed cache tool that is usually used to improve data access performance. But, it is interesting to see that the Go Wandering team also found another benefit of using it to save operational costs. How?

Just like any other modern SaaS (Software as a Service) nowadays, the Go Wandering system are orchestrated to work with many external services to do various things. For example, it requires a service to get the information of weather conditions, or to get the information of nearby facilities around certain places.

Depending on the subscription plan, these external services usually have certain access policy that either limits the number of API requests or just accepting any calls and increase the bills if it's a "pay as you go" plan. On both cases, having a strategy to minimize the calls to the external services will surely give possitive impact on the performance and the expense.
